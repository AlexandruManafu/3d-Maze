#3D Maze

The project was developed with the following:

Codeblocks 17.12

MinGW

Freeglut

Features:

The camera may be rotated, the yaw is adjusted with Q and E, the pitch is adjusted with F,R.

The camera may be translated with WASD, this does take into account the pitch and yaw.

A basic maze constructed out of cubes

Reset the camera with P, for a birds eye view press B.
